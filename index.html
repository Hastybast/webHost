About Slemani

Slemani, also known as Sulaymaniyah, is a vibrant city in Iraqi Kurdistan. Known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and breathtaking landscapes, Slemani is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking an authentic Kurdish experience.

Top Attractions

  • Goizha Mountain - Hike for stunning city views.
  • Amna Suraka - A museum showcasing Kurdish history.
  • Azadi Park - A tranquil green space for relaxation.

Kurdish Culture

Slemani is the cultural capital of Kurdistan, renowned for its music, poetry, and festivals. Be sure to explore the local cuisine, including dishes like biryani, dolma, and kebab.
